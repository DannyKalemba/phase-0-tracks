Welcome to the World of Monkeys

This is a page all about monkeys. The reasons they are awesome and the reasons they do not make good pets. Here is some info on Monkeys. Monkeys are primates that are indigenous to Africa Central America, and South America. Monkeys are classified into two subsections, New World Monkeys and Old World Monkeys. There are 264 known living species of monkeys throughout the world, and are generally considered to be among the most intelligent of mammals. Monkeys are very different from apes, most notably because monkeys have tails and apes do not.
